And I found one! But before we get into that, let me share with you some interesting (and scary!) information that I found online regarding oral health.

  • The oral cavity contains over 700 microbal species
  • the oral microbiota can be altered by a series of endogenous and exogenous factors including diet, smoking, alcohol, antibiotics and pregnancy
  • These alterations can disrupt the bacterial equilibrium in the oral cavity by increasing hamrful bacteria and decreasing beneficial ones, leading to a series of infectious diseases such as periodontal diseases
  • Sugar can lead to dental caries
  • Fiber and dairy help maintain a healthy balance in the oral microbiota
  • Smoking can alter oral microbiota by increasing acidity of saliva, depleting oxygen, and impairing host immunity
